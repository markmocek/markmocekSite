Session One:

     The party found themselves in a cave with one door. The cave was poorly lit by some candles, on a table, in the center of the room. On that table was a piece of paper with colored circles drawn in various locations.
     The group took the paper and entered the next room. In that room the party found a gnome, elf, human, orc, and tiefling standing near the far wall. The group attacked the opposing party. Genn, a man with wolf like qualities, engages in taunting the elf. Teah spent some of the battle looking for land mines, the hard way. The group quickly made use of the map they had previously discovered to avoid as many traps as possible. The spellcaster, D'Jango, used powerful magics against the enemy orc, ripping half of the orc's body apart.
     As the fight continued, Genn got close and personal with the elf Faelyn. Faelyn seemed to see something in Genn that caused some form of attraction. Genn returned this affection by mutilating Faelyn with his sword. In a fit of blind rage, the already wounded gnome attempted to leap at Genn with a land mine. Genn, who was holding Faelyn's lifeless body, barely managed to avoid the leaping gnome(CRITICAL 20). The gnome's face turned to horror as he collided with Faelyn, blowing their body parts all over the room. The tiefling, at seeing her party members die such horrific deaths, fell to her knees and sat there in shock.
     The group finished off the human who was still fighting and then finished off the devastated tiefling. Xavi healed some of D'Jango's injuries and began collecting body parts to bury the dead. The party decided to take a long rest.

Session Two:

     Teah found a piece of paper among the fallen enemy corpses. The document seemed to be a request to find a gem and included a map to a nearby town. The party decided to head for the town in hopes of finding and returning the gem to its owner, in return for a reward of course.
     As the party traveled to the town, they came across a circle where no plants were growing. The circle had a couple cow skeletons lined up in a row. The group decided to quickly move on towards the town.
     Upon arrival, the group learned that some of the local livestock was strangely disappearing, and a boy who lived on a cattle farm near the edge of the village had seen something mysterious. Genn decided to investigate further and went to find the boy in question, with Xavi trailing behind.
     D'Jango and Teah decided to go to the local inn where they met a barkeep named Fran. Fran, a woman of few words, gave the two keys to rooms on the second floor of the inn. Teah and D'Jango went up to their respective rooms where they discovered how incredibly disgusting the arrangements were. The beds had bugs and were just slabs of hay.
     Genn and Xavi, now at the boy's home on the farm, knocked on the door. Xavi flew to the roof of the house while Genn stood waiting for the door to open. A man opened the door. Genn inquired about the boy who saw something taking people's livestock. The man, nervous at seeing a man with wolf like features, tried to close the door. Genn placed his foot in the doorway to prevent it from closing. Genn pushed at the door as the man tried to push it closed. Genn heard the man call someone over inside of the house. As Genn pushed inside, he heard what sounded like someone running away from the house. The man fell to the ground as Genn forced his way into the room. Genn then took the pommel of his sword and hit the man upside the head. The man stopped moving aside from the slow up and down of his chest as he breathed. Genn then struck the man a second time, splitting his skull. The man let loose a quick, violent shake and then lay still. Xavi flew down to check on the man who had brain matter exposed from the fatal blow. Genn then went back towards the town in search of the boy.
     Unable to find the boy, Genn went to the inn where he had a small encounter with the barmaid Fran. The encounter ended when she pulled a knife and gently poked the tip into Genn's armpit, drawing blood. Genn then went upstairs and kicked a hole in the door to D'Jango's room. Teah, seeing Genn with his foot stuck in the door, pushed Genn causing him to break through the rest of the door and onto the floor. After a brief scuffle, Genn decided to sleep on D'Jango's floor while D'Jango and Teah went downstairs.
     Xavi, now back in town, watched a house where the boy had run to hide. Teah, meanwhile, went and stood outside the inn. A group of seven armed villagers left the house where the boy had gone and went towards the cow farm. Xavi and Teah followed.
     After discovering the man's corpse, the village militia took his body to bury him. At this time, Teah and Xavi appeared at the house. Teah informed the militia that a wolfman that may have caused the death is currently at the village inn. The militia moved to apprehend the man she mentioned. Xavi stayed behind while Teah followed the militia back.
     Back in town, the militia go to the room where Genn was staying and ask him to come with them. Genn agrees and peacefully follows the militia back to a house. Genn was then placed in a small cell in the basement of the house.
     Meanwhile, Xavi sees a creature going after the cows on the farm. Xavi uses magic to scare the deformed ugly creature, who then run into the forest. Xavi follows it to a clearing where two demonic creatures are sacrificing animals. Xavi spots a red gem and steals it by distracting the demons with a wall of water. Xavi begins running out of the forest with the gem.

Session Three:

     Xavi continued to run from his demon pursuers. Attempting to lose them and head for town, Xavi decided to hide among the branches of a tree. This worked for the smaller demon, but the two large ones were able to sense Xavi and the stone he carried. While the demons started searching the nearby area, Xavi used magic to cause a small distraction which allowed him to escape back to the town.
     Meanwhile, Genn remained in the cell beneath house. One of the militia, an older man named Hector, came down to discuss the death of the cow farmer. Genn attempted, without success, to deceive Hector with poorly constructed fabrications. Hector decided that it was in his best interest to get a confession before dealing with punishments, so he brought Teah and D'Jango to the house for questioning.
     Xavi arrived at the town just as the rest of the group was about to be questioned. Xavi noticed the smaller demon on the outskirts of the town looking for him. Xavi decided to mess with the small demon. Using magic, Xavi harassed the creature until to ran back off into the woods. Xavi then sat just outside the window of the house where the party awaited questioning.
     Hector questioned the group while a new man stood nearby. The man was not wearing armor, but instead had a crisp white tabard and a simple sword. The tabard had a picture of an orc, that looked less gruesome than most orcs, with what seemed like rainbow mist coming from its eyes. Unhappy with the results of the questioning, Hector decided to push harder to put the group against themselves. Hector told the group that he would execute someone for the crime and he was no longer worried who received the punishment. He also told the group that the small boy in the room with them had information to give regarding the murder. The group made remarks that brought the boy to tears before attempting to cast Suggestion (14) on Hector so he would let them go to the house by themselves. Hector decided that going to the house might not be such a terrible plan.
     Hector brought the group, the man in the white tabard, and the boy to the farm to "help investigate" the murder. While there Genn claimed to hear a noise near the treeline. Unconvinced, Hector let Genn walk with the man in the white tabard. Xavi who was previously eluding a militia man followed the group to the farm and sat on the roof holding the red gem he had recovered earlier.
     Now alone, Genn being outside and the boy being in another room of the house, Hector went for a hail Mary. Hector offered Teah and D'Jango money to kill Genn while Xavi played with the dark magic inside of the gem.
     Genn started walking back towards the house and, sensing an opportunity, quickly took the sword belonging to the man in the white tabard. Genn simultaneously slashed him across the chest while pulling the sword from its sheath on the man's hip. The group, along with Hector, ran outside to see what was happening. Genn stood over the man, whose tabard began to turn red from the wound on his chest, while shouting "He attacked me, I defended myself!" Teah drew her bow as the man stood up infront of Genn. The man held out his hand as the sword vanish from Genn’s hand and reappeared in his own. Then man then closed his eyes as his clothing began to glow with white power. With a quick flash the cloth clothing was replaced with plate armor and the one-handed sword with a larger two-hander. Teah fired an arrow at Genn as the man prepared for combat.
     Suddenly, a large demon came out of the forest. It was one of the two that had been pursuing Xavi. The now armored man turned to face it as Hector charged the beast. Xavi, now intoxicated with the magic of the gem, began creating magic writings in the sky and a magical fountain. Genn took the moment of distraction to flee into the forest, where he ran into the second of the two demons. The first demon moved towards the advancing Hector and shoved its massive fingers into his chest. Hector's cried quieted as his body began to wither as though the very essence of his life were being drained.
     Xavi, seeing the magic flowing within the demons as possible fuel for his new gem, hugged the demon. Sensing the gem, the demon pulled the small pixie, Xavi, from his head and began beating him into the ground. Seeing the power of the demon, Teah fled from the fight.
     D'Jango moved into the house and positioned himself near the window facing the demon, who was still beating Xavi. D'Jango then fired off some spells, hitting the demon. The demon did not stop. It beat Xavi to the point of death, and then absorbed the small creatures magic into the gem that it had reclaimed. Finished, it turned its sights to D'Jango. As the demon crashed through the side of the house, D'Jango cast Invisibility and fled the fight. Genn, who was in the forest being chased by the second demon, eventually outpaced the demon and fled the fight as well.

Session Four:

     Our new party is heading down a road looking for a town. The party consists of Halt, a nobleman and archer; Selene, Halt's loyal bodyguard and a cleric; Dax, a bard; and Kurzick, a revenant and a blood hunter.
     As the party walks down the path, a man walks up to them. He tells them to turn back for there is nothing but danger ahead. The party, seeing a chance to save the day, head towards the town.
     On their way to the town, the sky began to darken. The group looked up at the unnaturally appearing clouds just as a beam of light broke through above the nearby town. There was a bright flash and the party saw an angelic figure suddenly appear and descend on the town. With another bright flash, the figure disappeared along with the dark clouds.
     Looking back to each other, Selene realized that everyone was devastated by the explosion of light(CRITICAL 1). Her party members were torn to shreds, but seemed to be in so much shock that they acted as if nothing was wrong. Selene tried to help the party only to eventually discover that there was nothing wrong with them. Selene cast Remove Curse lvl 4 to return her vision to normal.
     When the party reached the town it was completely destroyed. Buildings were burning and bone that seemed to have been burned clean, despite a lack of black ashe. The party moved past the town and into the nearby forest only to discover a severely wounded demon. The party made quick work of it with Selene only accidentally shooting a party member(CRITICAL 1) once with her crossbow and Kurzick finishing off the demon.
     With the demon dead, the party decided to try and find where it came from. Some tracks were found and the party began following them deeper into the forest. Halt, tired with the loud and slow pace of his allies, decided to pull off and find the demon's origin on his own. Unfortunately, some cultists were searching for possible sacrifices and found Halt. They knocked him out and took him to one of their sacrificial circles.
     The rest of the party quickly discovers Halt's absence and tries to find him. Their search bring them to a cave entrance where they hear chanting. The party enters the cave to find a group of cultists chanting over six people who seem to be unconscious. Seeing that Halt is one of them, the party burst into action and kill all but one of the cultists. While the group interrogates their captured cultist, Kurzick examines the dagger of power he found on the cultist performing the sacrifice. The dagger almost seems to have a consciousness as he holds it in his hands(CRITICAL 1). Meanwhile, the captured cultist seems to be incredibly unitelligent and is killed by a powerful blow to the head from Selene's warhammer. The party decides to take this opportunity to rest.

Session Seven:

     The party experience a piece of The War of Purity as though they were the angels themselves. The angels are descending on a mortal city that seems to be filled with all variations of the mortal races. The six Archangels Adonael, Imperious, Guriel, Sidriel, Hadriel, and Karael quickly sensed the taint of demons among the mortals. They decide to split up to close the portals as soon as possible, with Adonael taking on the largest portal.
     Imperious and Sidriel quickly headed to one of the portals to find hordes of demons and two of the Lords of Hell stepping through the portal. Luckily the great demons were still incredibly weak from pushing themselves through the portal and were quickly dispatched. Guriel and Karael quickly dealt with their portal and the Lords of Hell that were coming through it. Hadriel, who braved his portal alone, also managed to close a portal and destroy a forming Lord of Hell before it was able to completely enter the mortal plane.
     It was as the Archangels closed the portals that they realized that they could no longer feel Adonael’s presence. The five quickly flew to the large portal that Adonael had gone to. With the portal, the largest of all the ones in the city, still open and leaking demons, the Archangels decided to take the battle to Hell.
     Once through the portal, the Archangels felt a glimmer of Adonael's presence. Unfortunately, there was also a legion of demons pressing towards them along with the remnants of the Lords of Hell. Seeing no other choice, the Archangels began fighting off the demons. Angels poured in from the portal to the mortal plane and fell into rank among the five great Archangels. Each angel killed many demons before falling, but the angels were greatly outnumbered. The power of the Archangels allowed the holy army to hold off the demons and that final glimmer of Adonael vanished from the Archangel's awareness. It was at this moment that an even larger collection of demons could be seen marching towards the fight.
     The Archangels chose to pull back before they were completely overrun. The Archangels manned the opening of the portal as the lesser angels began making their escape back to the mortal plane. During the fight at the portal, the encroaching demonic army made contact with the remaining angels. The Archangels prepared to leave when they noticed a familiar figure flying towards them. It appeared to be Adonael, although somewhat battered and discolored. He appeared to have black wings instead of the stark white of his fellow Archangels. His white and gold armor also seemed to be darker and damaged. Fearing his safety, Karael flew out to Adonael so she could help him escape. Upon reaching Adonael, he grabbed her and dragged her to the ground. The other Archangels, surprised by the sudden act of aggression prepared themselves for whatever may follow. Adonael, still holding Karael by the throat, looked to his fellow Archangels. His eyes, which were a bright blue, now shone with a dark red.
     Adonael stated that the their quest to keep out the darkness has always been pointless and was futile since the disappearance of their father Nahir. He said that Melphas has given him even more power, a new name of Asmoday, and they should join him. They refused and Karael told Asmoday to have faith that Nahir would return. Asmoday seemed annoyed by her call for faith and lifted his sword. Seeing this act of aggression, Hadriel quickly put his protection on Karael. Incredible pain filled Hadriel's body as he felt the pain meant for Karael. Asmoday smirked, and told Hadriel that his power was futile to protect anyone, least of all Karael. Hadriel felt his protective connect lift from Karael's form as Asmoday drove his sword back into her body. White angelic blood started dripping off the blade that was protruding out of Karael's back. She looked to the four remaining Archangels and use her remaining strength to send them through the portal and close it.
     The party felt themselves being pulled back out of the vision and to Folwin’s side. There were not sure how long it had been, but just as they were returning the human that was lying to Folwin under the portal seemed to be waking up. He said his name was Stan.

Selene examined the guard and found that he had been killed. The guards body had been propped up against the wall with wire to that it appeared as though he were standing guard. Selene noticed that Halt had not yet joined her and when behind the shop to look for him. She saw the purple cloaked man walking away with Halt thrown over his shoulder. She attacks the cloaked man. The man quickly drops Halt and runs off into the crowded city. Selene heals Halt and takes him back to the inn.

Session Thirteen:

     The group prepares for their travel to Stiria. Stan gives the group enough supplies for their journey there and wishes them good luck. Stan mentions that he fears the council may be corrupt due to all the odd things happening around the city. Halt mentions that the dwarven councilor may have had something to do with the jewel shops. Stan takes note and promises to look into it. The group heads out.
     During their travels to Stiria the group discovered a small squirrel under attack of a hawk. The group ignored it, possible due to either a love of hawks or a hate for squirrels. The group later came across a dwarf and samurai. The two wanted to be paid for allowing the party’s wagon to pass. The group refused and battle ensued. The party seemed to be beating the duo when two more of the bandits appeared. These new two claimed to be the brothers of the dwarf and human that the party had killed. The dwarf was thick and heavily muscled while the human was wearing leather armor and held a katana. The party engaged the two in combat. They were significantly tougher than their respective brothers. The party managed to triumph due to the Banish spell used by Elfy, but Halt lost his life.
     Halt entered a dark area as the life left his body. In this place was a cloaked figure in a seat made of bones. Behind the figure seemed to be a golden staff, but it seemed faded and forgotten. The figure's body was covered with a faded grey cloak. As Halt stood there, the figure tilted its head. Halt felt a strong hand grasp his shoulder as he was pulled from the room. Halt woke up on the ground as Selene used her abilities to bring Halt back from death.

Session Fourteen:

     Halt began to feel ill as the group continued their travel. It was at this point that he realized that the poison inflicted on him by the purple figures was starting to take effect. While trying to figure out a way to deal with the effects of the poison, the party noticed riders gaining behind them. The riders were quickly gaining on the party and appeared to be wearing two different colored hoods. The one closest to the caravan was wearing a familiar dark purple hood, while the one trailing behind was wearing a dark red. The two caught up to the part and began fighting. Unsure on what to make of the situation, the part stopped and prepared to fight. Halt shot an arrow at the purple hooded assassin, catching him in the knee. The red hooded assassin then sprinted after the purple one, who was now trying to flee, and tackled him. The red assassin began repeatedly stabbing the purple in the chest until he was satisfied that the other was dead.
     The party approached the red assassin. The man said his name was Davit and that he was part of The Sarasota Assassins guild. He also stated that the purple man was of the Mogiz assassins guild. Davit explained that the Mogiz seemed to have a thing for killing noblemen and that the Sarasota did not appreciate it since that is where most of their contracts came from. Korvak, learning that the dead assassin was probably after Halt, took this opportunity to stomp the dead man's upper body with his foot. Halt told Davit that he would gladly pay him for an antidote. Davit collected some poison from the dead assassin’s remains and constructed a temporary antidote for Halt to use until he could make a better one. Davit rode off to prepare for any Mogiz that he hoped would be following Halt.
     The party continue traveling again and made it to the outskirts of the city of Stiria. While traveling, they noticed the large groups of people seemed to be leaving the city. The party discovered a small home that seemed to have a blacksmith's shop. The group approached the smith who called himself Aldis. The group inquired about the state of the kingdom. Aldis told the group that he did not believe the king was an evil man, but something was causing him to execute common criminals for lesser crimes. Aldis promised he would make something special for the group if they managed to help the kingdom without killing its king, Galen.
     The group then entered the city, except for Korvak who remain in a nearby wooded area, and witnessed the king's executions. The party noted that the king executed the criminals himself and did so with a swords that gave off an undead presence. The part went to an inn to rest and prepare for whatever would come next. Selene began gambling with some people in the inn. Elfy pulled out his dragon chess set and entered a battle of strategy with one of the people within the inn. Halt tried to pickpocket a man sitting at the bar. Unfortunately, he was discovered and taken by the guards to the city prison in preparation for the next execution.
