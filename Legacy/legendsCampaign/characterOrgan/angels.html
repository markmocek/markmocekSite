Nahir, Lord of Light

     Nahir was one of the two original being, along with his brother Melphas, and creator of the angels and Heaven. Nahir worked in unison with Melphas to create the mortal world Terra. Nahir has not been seen by the angels or mortal races since his disappearance during The Darkening.

Adonael, Archangel of Purity

     Adonael was the first of Nahir's creations and the most powerful. He fought the demons of Hell during the The War of Purity and when on to confront Melphas himself. Unfortunately for the archangel, Melphas's dark powers corrupted Adonael and caused him to turn on his fellow angels as the create called Asmoday. In the final battle of the war, Asmoday confronted the archangels who had enter Hell's gates. He killed Karael while she secured an escape for the remaining angels out of Hell.

Imperious, Archangel of Valor

     Imperious was a zealous fighter for Nahir. Frequently seen by Adonael's side, the archangel was distraught by his corruption. Once The War of Purity was drawn to a close, Imperious was left the sole archangel in Heaven. He has since sought ways back into the mortal world of Terra so he can cleanse the world of darkness and push for a final assault on Hell.
